Guidelines: Step-by-Step Tutorial for PM Internship Scheme

Follow these steps to navigate the website, register, and apply for internships.

  1. Step 1: Visit the Home Page

    Start by visiting the home page of the PM Internship Scheme website. Here, you can see the banner with the scheme's title and tagline, and the navigation bar with links to different sections.

    Go to Home
  2. Step 2: Login or Sign Up

    If you already have an account, click "Login" to enter your credentials. If you're new, click "Sign Up" to create an account. Fill in your details including name, email, skills, and location.

    Login Sign Up
  3. Step 3: Choose Your Registration Type

    After logging in, choose whether you are a student or a company. Click on "Student Registration" if you are a student looking for internships, or "Company Registration" if you are a company offering internships.

    Student Registration Company Registration
  4. Step 4: Fill Out the Registration Form

    Complete the registration form with accurate information. For students: provide your name, email, skills, and location. For companies: provide company name, role, skills required, and location. Submit the form to register.

  5. Step 5: Check Your Profile

    After registration, you will be redirected to your profile page. Here, you can view and edit your information. Make sure all details are correct.

    View Profile
  6. Step 6: Explore Other Sections

    Use the navigation bar to explore other sections like "About Scheme" for more information, "Guidelines" for this tutorial, and "Contact Us" for support.

    About Scheme Contact Us
  7. Step 7: Apply or Post Internships

    Once registered, students can apply for internships, and companies can post internship opportunities. Check back regularly for updates.
